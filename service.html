Designed for
If you and your friends are interested in Asian food delivery, this is the perfect option for you!
Let us know your address and choose the specialty dish that you would like to try out. Then you need to do is to wait for the authentic food delivered at the comfort of your home. Make sure to order beforehands via our contact us link for the future deliveries.
If you spot something on our specialty menu and are interested in making your own version, this is the option for you!
We will shop for your portion of the ingredients and send along the recepe for the dish on the delivery day. If there is additional spices that you are looking for, drop a note in your order to let us know and we will bring them to your house.
If you are looking for a catering service for Asian food potluck, this is perfect for you!
Place the order ahead of time via our contact us link or give us a call. We will send you the menu of food available for catering and confirms the details for you. Because there are only 3 chefs, we are not expecting to serve a party over 10 people currently (sorry!)
